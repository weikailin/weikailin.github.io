I am Wei-Kai Lin, a Ph.D. student
in Computer Science at Cornell.

I am interested in cryptography and theoretical computer science in general.

My research focuses on designing efficient oblivious algorithms. My recent work OptORAMa shows how to construct a logarithmic overhead ORAM. Since it is well-known that any ORAM scheme must incur at least logarithmic overhead, OptORAMa is asymptotically optimal.
